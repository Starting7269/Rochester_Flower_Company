Craft cannabis for real people.

Rochester Flower Company isn't a brand or a business. It's just one grower in Rochester, NY working in small batches to make good flower accessible to people who genuinely rely on it. No storefront, no hype ‚Äì just plants, craft, and community.

How this all started

This didn't begin as a "project." It started because I saw people in my life struggling to afford the thing that helped them function, sleep, heal, or feel like themselves. Meanwhile, the legal market flooded with corporate branding, high prices, and flower that didn't feel like medicine.

Here's something that bothers me: Some commercial growers don't dry, cure, or store properly‚Äîletting the terpenes off-gas. Then they spray synthetic terpenes back on to fake quality. That's not medicine. That's not cool.

So I decided to grow the way I wish the world grew: small batches, clean inputs, honest curing, no shortcuts, and no sales ‚Äì just trustworthy flower, shared responsibly with adults who need it.

What I believe in

  • Small batch: Good cannabis isn't made at scale. It comes from attention, the kind you only get when the entire grow is in the hands of one person. I typically run 2-4 plants per cycle.
  • Clean environments: Indoor tents, coco/perlite, tuned LEDs, and obsessive environmental management. Temp, humidity, VPD, DLI. Everything is intentional.
  • Slow dry, real cure: Fast drying ruins good work. Every harvest gets the time it deserves so the terpene profile actually survives long enough to be appreciated.
  • No ego, no hype: I don't compete, I don't chase trends, and I don't pretend this is a business. I just grow because it helps people and because I love it.

What this is (and isn't)

Everything I grow is gifted, not sold. New York law allows adults 21+ to gift cannabis to other adults ‚Äì without payment, trades, services, or anything of value involved. I follow that fully.

I don't sell cannabis.
I don't run a commercial operation.
I don't offer medical advice.
I don't diagnose, treat, or prescribe.

This is a home garden, one I take seriously, work hard on, and am proud to share with people who truly benefit from it.

Who this is for

  • People who use cannabis as real medicine
  • People who appreciate well-grown flower
  • People who value honesty and transparency
  • People who can't always afford dispensary prices
  • People who believe craft matters

Who this isn't for

  • Anyone under 21 (strictly enforced)
  • People looking for commercial transactions
  • Those seeking medical advice or diagnosis
  • Anyone uncomfortable with the personal, informal nature of gifting
  • People building a stash rather than using for genuine need

Where I operate

I operate exclusively in Rochester, NY and surrounding areas where I can personally verify compliance and build genuine relationships. This isn't scalable, and that's intentional.

This is a garden, not a distribution network. Quality and care don't scale, and I'm not trying to make them.

The human behind the plants

It's just me: a husband, a pet dad, a spreadsheet addict, and a slightly obsessive plant nerd. What started as a hobby became a personal mission: grow the best flower I can, share it responsibly, and make the whole thing feel human again.

One day I might put more of myself here. Photos, stories, the journey. But for now, just know that everything you see comes from one pair of hands, in one home, in one city.

Where to go next

I don't have big plans to scale or turn this into something commercial. The goal is simple: keep the garden healthy, keep learning, keep helping, and keep sharing. If I were allowed to dream for a moment, I'd love to see my passion for helping animals and providing medicinal plants to people collide.
