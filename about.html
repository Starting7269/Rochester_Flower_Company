About Rochester Flower Company

This isn’t a brand launch. It’s just my home grow, a lot of love, and some flower that keeps finding its way into the hands of people who need it.

Hi. I’m the grower.

Rochester Flower Company is basically the fancy name I gave to my deeply overbuilt home grow in Rochester, NY. It’s small, personal, and very much powered by spreadsheets, timers, and a slightly ridiculous amount of care.

I started RFC because I kept meeting people who used cannabis as medicine—sleep, pain, anxiety, appetite—and then had to play “which bill do I not pay this month” to afford it. That didn’t sit right with me.

What I actually do

A few times a year, I run small, carefully dialed-in grows. When a harvest is ready, the flower gets trimmed, cured, and then quietly gifted to adults who genuinely need it and can’t easily afford consistent, safe supply.

  • No online store.
  • No price list.
  • No “drops.”

Just plants, people, and a little bit of mutual aid.

Things I care about

  • People first – The whole point is to make someone’s day/life a bit easier.
  • Craft over hype – Clean inputs, consistent process, no “mystery bag” energy.
  • Honesty – Clear about what this is and what it isn’t. No pretending to be a dispensary.
  • Respect – For the law, for the plant, and for the folks who trust me with their medicine.

Important notes (the non-fun but necessary part)

Rochester Flower Company is a personal, small-scale home grow in New York. I do not sell cannabis products. Any cannabis shared through this project is gifted free of charge and only to adults 21+.

I’m not a doctor, pharmacist, or therapist. Nothing on this site is medical advice—please talk to a licensed professional about your health and treatment options.
