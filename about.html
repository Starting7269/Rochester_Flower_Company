About Us

Rochester Flower Company is a small batch cannabis grower based in Rochester, New York. Our passion lies in cultivating artisan strains with care and precision, prioritizing quality, sustainability, and the local community. Each plant is grown with attention to detail and a deep respect for the craft.

We're not just growers — we're stewards of a mindful, plant-based lifestyle. While we don’t sell products directly through our website due to legal reasons, we hope our story inspires a deeper appreciation for the artistry behind cannabis cultivation.

Why Cannabis

When I was much younger, I was diagnosed with a chronic neurological condition. For years, I was stuck in a cycle of prescription medications—some prescribed just to manage the side effects of others. It felt like a never-ending nesting doll of drugs. Even at a young age, I could sense that this approach was deeply flawed.

At the time, states like California were only beginning to explore the potential for medicinal use of cannabis. Meanwhile, other countries outside the U.S. were seeing real success treating my condition with THC and CBD. That led to a very honest conversation with my neurologist. To paraphrase his words: “I can't tell you to break federal law, but if we were in a country where cannabis was legal, I would absolutely recommend it for you.”

We started the process of tapering off the prescription drugs. I transitioned to using cannabis as my primary treatment—and I felt better than I ever had. That experience changed everything for me. It opened my eyes to the truth that sometimes doing what’s right for yourself means stepping outside the lines others have drawn.

That realization lit a fire in me—a lifelong mission to cultivate and share the best medicine the world is still waking up to: cannabis.
