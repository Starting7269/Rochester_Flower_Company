About Rochester Flower Company

A tiny grow with a big heart. Small-batch craft cannabis, gifted to people who truly need it.

Who We Are

Rochester Flower Company is a personal, small-scale cultivation project based in Rochester, New York. It started with a simple idea: medicine shouldn’t be out of reach for the people who rely on it most.

This is not a commercial operation. There’s no storefront, no checkout, no shopping cart. Just a grower who loves plants, science, and helping people who genuinely benefit from cannabis.

What We Do

We grow limited runs of high-quality cannabis using clean inputs, controlled environments, and a craft-first mindset. When harvest comes, the flower is gifted to adults who:

  • Use cannabis as part of their wellness or pain-management routine, and
  • Struggle to afford consistent, dispensary-priced products.

Every gram is gifted—not sold—and always with discretion and respect.

Values

  • Compassion – People first, always.
  • Craft – Small batches, careful hands, serious attention to detail.
  • Transparency – Clear about what we are and what we are not.
  • Respect – For the plant, the law, and the people we serve.

Important Notes

Rochester Flower Company does not sell cannabis products. All cannabis is gifted free of charge to adults 21+ only. We do not make medical claims or offer medical advice. Please consult a licensed healthcare professional about your health and treatment options.
