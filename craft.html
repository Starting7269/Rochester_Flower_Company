How We Grow

Craft cannabis is about care, not volume. Here’s how each plant gets treated like it matters—because it does.

Environment & Medium

  • Indoor, controlled environment with LED lighting.
  • Coco coir + perlite medium for oxygen-rich, consistent root zones.
  • Automated watering and air-based root support for steady hydration and aeration.

The goal is consistency: stable temperatures, dialed-in vapor pressure deficit (VPD), and predictable nutrition so the plant can focus on thriving.

Nutrition & Care

  • Balanced, time-release nutrition tailored for cannabis.
  • Regular visual checks for plant health, posture, and vigor.
  • Gentle training to maximize light exposure without stress.

Every plant is checked by human eyes—not just meters and timers. Craft means knowing each plant as an individual.

Drying

After harvest, plants are hung whole in a controlled environment aiming for around 60°F and 60% relative humidity whenever possible.

This slow dry protects terpenes and keeps the smoke smooth, avoiding the harshness that comes from rushing the process.

Curing

Once stems snap and the outside of the buds feels dry to the touch, flower is bucked down and moved into curing containers.

Stable, cool conditions and consistent humidity help the buds finish their transformation into something truly enjoyable.

Why It Matters

People who rely on cannabis for relief deserve more than random, harsh, or inconsistent flower. By keeping things small and intentional, Rochester Flower Company focuses on delivering medicine that’s flavorful, smooth, and trustworthy—every time.
