Small-Batch Craft Cannabis.
Grown by a normal person.

I’m a one-person grow in Rochester, NY with a couple of tents, too many spreadsheets, and a soft spot for people who genuinely need cannabis as a medicine.

I grow medicine-grade flower in tiny batches and gift it to adults who actually use it as medicine. No storefront, no menu, no hype—just plants, care, and community.

Send an email What is this project?

I don’t sell cannabis. Everything is gifted, not sold, and only to adults 21+ in line with New York’s personal-grow rules.

Why this exists

Some people use cannabis the same way other folks use an inhaler, a heating pad, or a good night’s sleep: it keeps them functional and sane. Then the prices hit, and suddenly their “medicine” is a luxury item.

Rochester Flower Company is my way of pushing back a little: grow small, grow clean, and quietly hand good flower to people who actually need it.

How I grow

Indoor tents, coco/perlite, dialed-in LEDs, controlled temp/RH, and a very unhealthy love of VPD charts.

Outdoor soil/perlite. Mother Nature handles lighting and environment and I keep them fed and watered.

All plants are slow dried, cured properly, and I obsess over the details most people never see—but definitely feel.
