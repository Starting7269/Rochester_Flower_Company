Small Batch Craft Cannabis,
Grown by Someone Who Genuinely Cares.

I’m a home grower in Rochester, NY with a couple of tents, too many notes, and a soft spot for people who rely on medicinal plants.

I grow clean, slow-cured flower in small batches and gift it to adults 21+ who truly benefit from it.
No storefront. No hype. No pressure. Just plants, craft, and community.

What is this project?

I don’t sell anything. Everything is gifted, not sold, and only gifted to adults 21+ in compliance with New York’s personal grow laws as documented in NY Penal Law § 222.05.

Why this exists

Some people use plants the same way other folks use an inhaler, a heating pad, or a good night’s sleep: it keeps them functional and sane. Then the prices hit, and suddenly their “medicine” is a luxury item.

Rochester Flower Company is my way of pushing back a little: grow small, grow clean, and quietly hand good flower to people who actually need it.

How I grow

Indoor tents, coco/perlite, dialed-in LEDs, controlled temp/RH, and a very unhealthy love of VPD charts.

Outdoor soil/perlite. Mother Nature handles lighting and environment and I keep them fed and watered.

All plants are slow dried, cured properly, and I obsess over the details most people never see but definitely taste and feel.

The human behind the plants

RFC isn’t a “brand team.” It’s just me, my wife, our very spoiled pets, and a home grow that got a little out of hand in the best way possible.
