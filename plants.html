Understanding the Timeline

Cannabis cultivation takes time. Here's what a typical grow cycle looks like, so you understand why there might be gaps between harvests:

Typical Grow Timeline:

Seedling: 1-2 weeks
Vegetative: 4-8 weeks
Flowering: 8-10 weeks
Drying: 10-14 days
Curing: 3-8 weeks minimum
Total: 4-6 months from seed to ready flower

This is a garden, not a dispensary. Real craft takes real time.
