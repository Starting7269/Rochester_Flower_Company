Why We Grow Small-Batch

March 2025

Small-batch growing isn't just a trendy label for us—it’s the foundation of our entire approach. We believe that great cannabis comes from care, and care takes time, attention, and intention.

By focusing on smaller grows, we can give each plant the hands-on treatment it deserves. We're able to fine-tune environmental conditions, closely monitor growth, and spot issues early—ensuring each crop gets exactly what it needs to thrive.

This also allows us to experiment, iterate, and improve constantly. We're not bound by mass-production rules or rushing plants to market. We grow at the plant’s pace—not the industry's.

When you consume something we've grown, you can trust it was made with purpose. It's better for the environment, better for the consumer, and honestly, better for our souls. That’s why small-batch isn’t a limitation—it’s our superpower.
