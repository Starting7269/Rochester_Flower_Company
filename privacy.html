Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

This isn't a data harvesting operation. I'm just one person with some tents and plants. Here's what happens to your information when you contact me.

What Information I Collect

If you use the contact form, I collect what you give me:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • Your location (general area)
  • Whatever you write in the message field

I also use sessionStorage (browser-based, temporary) to remember that you've verified your age. This clears automatically when you close your browser.

That's it. I don't use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any of that stuff.

How I Use Your Information

Your contact information is used for exactly one thing: responding to your message.

I don't:

  • Sell your information
  • Share it with third parties (except the ones mentioned below)
  • Use it for marketing
  • Add you to email lists
  • Store it in databases

Once I've responded to your inquiry, I delete your information unless there's a specific reason to keep it (like ongoing conversation or a business relationship).

Third-Party Services

I use a few services to make the website work:

FormSubmit (Form Processing)

When you submit the contact form, it goes through FormSubmit.co to reach my email. They handle the submission and send it to me. I don't control their servers or their practices.

FormSubmit Privacy Policy

Cloudflare (Security & DNS)

My site uses Cloudflare for security, DDoS protection, and DNS management. They may collect technical information like IP addresses for security purposes.

Cloudflare Privacy Policy

GitHub Pages (Hosting)

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages. They may collect technical information for service operation.

GitHub Privacy Statement

Data Retention

Contact form submissions: I keep them until your inquiry is resolved, then delete them. Usually that's a few days or weeks at most.

Age verification: Stored in your browser's sessionStorage only. Automatically deleted when you close your browser.

Everything else: There is no "everything else." I'm not running analytics or storing logs beyond what my hosting provider does for basic operations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion: Email me and I'll delete your information immediately
  • Request access: Ask what information I have about you (probably just an email thread)
  • Correct information: If I got something wrong, let me know
  • Opt out: Just don't use the contact form

Since I'm not storing information in databases or using it for ongoing purposes, these rights are pretty straightforward.

Security

I take reasonable precautions to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption (your connection to the site is secure)
  • Cloudflare's security features (DDoS protection, WAF)
  • FormSubmit's security measures (for form processing)
  • No plain text storage of sensitive information

That said, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Don't send me your Social Security number, credit card info, or anything like that. The contact form is for basic inquiries, not sensitive data.

Children's Privacy

This site is for adults 21 and older only. I don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 21.

If you're under 21, you shouldn't be here, and you definitely shouldn't be filling out the contact form.

Changes to This Policy

If I make changes to this privacy policy, I'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

I'm not planning on making this more complicated than it needs to be, but if something changes with how the site works, I'll update this accordingly.

Contact About Privacy

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how I handle your information, you can reach me through the contact form.

I'm just one person running a home grow, not a legal department, but I'll do my best to answer your questions.
