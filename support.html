Support the Garden

Rochester Flower Company is a small personal home grow in Rochester, NY. There's no storefront, no menu, and no checkout. It's just me, a couple of tents, way too many spreadsheets, and a genuine commitment to people who rely on cannabis as medicine.

If you enjoy the photos, the nerdy details, or the idea of making good flower a little more accessible, you can chip in to help keep the garden going.

Support via Buy Me a Coffee

What your support helps with

Donations don't turn this into a business. They just make it a little easier to keep doing this the right way instead of the cheap way.

  • New genetics, seeds, and clones for future runs
  • Growing mediums like coco, perlite, soil, and the "let's see what happens" experiments
  • Upgrades to lights, fans, filters, and environmental controls
  • Electricity to keep the tents running safely and consistently
  • Better documentation and photography equipment so you can actually see what's going on

Other ways to support (that cost $0)

  • Share the site with a friend who nerds out about plants as much as you do
  • Send feedback, questions, or ideas for strains and experiments
  • Be cool about the fact this is a personal home grow, not a dispensary
  • Spread the word about craft cannabis and why it matters

What this project is (and isn't)

Rochester Flower Company is a personal, small-scale home grow in New York. Cannabis is not sold here. If cannabis is ever gifted, it is only:

  • To adults 21+ under New York's personal use and gifting rules
  • Without any payment, trade, service, or other exchange of value for the flower itself
  • In places where possession is legal (not in schools or other restricted locations)
  • To recipients who are also compliant with local laws

Any money you send via Buy Me a Coffee is a voluntary donation to support the garden and content, not payment for cannabis, products, or services. This is crucial: donations support the project (seeds, soil, lights, documentation), not the cannabis itself.

No medical advice is offered, and nothing here is meant to diagnose, treat, or cure anything. I'm not a doctor—talk to a licensed healthcare professional about whether cannabis is right for you.

Nothing on this page is legal or financial advice – it's just one home grower being transparent about what this project is and isn't.
